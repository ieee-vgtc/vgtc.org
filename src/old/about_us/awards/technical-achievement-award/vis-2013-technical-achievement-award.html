Technical Achievement Award: 
The Visualization Technical Achievement Award

The 2013 Visualization Technical Achievement Award goes to Kwan-Liu Ma, University of California at Davis, in recognition of his seminal work in large-scale data visualization and new visualization techniques. His work has stimulated research in new fields of visualization. The IEEE Visualization & Graphics Technical Community (VGTC) is pleased to award Kwan-Liu Ma the 2013 Visualization Technical Achievement Award.
