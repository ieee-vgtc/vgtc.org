Technical Achievement Award: 
The Visualization Career Award

The 2013 Visualization Technical Achievement Award goes to Gregory M. Nielson, Arizona State University, in recognition of seminal research in geometry/topology-based modeling algorithms for visualization and for co-founding the Visualization Conference and TVCG. The IEEE Visualization & Graphics Technical Community (VGTC) is pleased to award Gregory M. Nielson the 2013 Visualization Career Award.
