Technical Achievement Award: 
The Virtual Reality Technical Achievement Award

The 2012 Virtual Reality Technical Achievement Award goes to Dieter Schmalstieg of Graz University of Technology, Austria, in recognition of his seminal contributions to Augmented Reality. Prof. Schmalstieg has been working on Augmented Reality, graphics and visualization problems for almost 20 years. The IEEE VGTC is pleased to award Dieter Schmalstieg the 2012 Virtual Reality Technical Achievement Award.
