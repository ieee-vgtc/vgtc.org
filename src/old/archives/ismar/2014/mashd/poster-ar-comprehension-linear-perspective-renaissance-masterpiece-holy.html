Authors: 
Giovanni Landi
DOI Bookmark:

Abstract: 
This work shows how augmented reality can help the comprehension of the geometric construction of the perspective in the Holy Trinity fresco (Masaccio, 1426) in the church of Santa Maria Novella in Florence, opening new scenarios in the demonstration of the subtle underlying geometric principles.
