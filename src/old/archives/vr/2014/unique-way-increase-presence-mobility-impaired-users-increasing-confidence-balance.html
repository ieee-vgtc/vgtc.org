Authors: 
Rongkai Guo, Gayani Samaraweera, John Quarles
DOI Bookmark: 
http://dx.doi.org/10.1109/VR.2014.6802059
Abstract: 
To investigate presence in mobility impaired persons, we replicated the classic virtual pit experiment and included a responsive full body avatar (or lack thereof) as a 3D user interface. We recruited from two different populations: mobility impaired persons and healthy persons as a control. Results give insight into differences between healthy and mobility impaired users??? experience of presence in virtual environments.
