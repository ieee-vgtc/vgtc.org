Authors: 
Lukas Gruber, Tobias Langlotz, Pradeep Sen, Tobias Hollerer, Dieter Schmalstieg
DOI Bookmark: 
http://dx.doi.org/10.1109/VR.2014.6802044
Abstract: 
In this work, we present an improved radiance transfer sampling approach for probeless photometric registration, which combines adaptive sampling in image and visibility space with robust caching of radiance transfer to yield real time framerates for photorealistic AR scenes with dynamically changing scene geometry and environment lighting.
