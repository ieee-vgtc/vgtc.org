Missed a session? Looking for a great way to continue talking to the presenter of a paper?  Check out the these archives for presentation slides, videos, meta data, and more. Note that paper PDFs, which require a subscription, are not archived at vgtc.org, but are cross linked to the IEEE Digital Library.

Videos can also be found on the VGTCommunity Vimeo site at https://vimeo.com/vgtcommunity. Check back regularly as the archives are updated throughout the year.
